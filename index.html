Who I am

Virtual reality could be the next cultural leap on par with television and potentially film. I'm a graduate student in computer science (at Pace University) who is obsessed with how this new technology could hit its tipping point.
Video games are still the height of user immersion. If you play any of the current iterations of the top franchises available today, if they're not completely adverse to fantasy, sports, or violence, (or video games as a whole, for that matter), your awareness can be utterly encapsulated into vast and highly detailed virtual landscapes.
Topics that elude the grasp of the auteur are theoretically at the disposal of creators of new media, but both games and film are the key to creating impactful work.
