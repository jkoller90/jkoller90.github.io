Preview for November: "I'm visiting my folks' house, which is still ostensibly considered my house. My wallet was either stolen or it entered a blackhole. At any rate, its disappearance sparked a need for me to retire to the colder northern Yorktown, New York. Now, as I listen to Grimes and let nippy air filter in through the window, I'm reminded of the hugely influential difference between my parents and I: it's much easier to be relaxed if you are near retirement. It is also irrational but seemingly inherent that I'll get frustrated by the difference in our anxiety levels: my hairline is experiencing a recession in the wake of rent-fueled cortisol release as they crank the heat and blissfully watch Ellen DeGeneres."
